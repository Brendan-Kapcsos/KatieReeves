WHAT KATIE
STANDS FOR

THE RIGHT EXPERIENCE

Katie has been serving Alpharetta residents for over three decades. She was appointed to the Alpharetta Tree Commission in 1991, moved to the Alpharetta Planning Commission in 1995, and finally was elected to the Fulton County Board of Education in 1998, where she served until 2022. There is no candidate more uniquely qualified to serve us on City Council than Katie Reeves.

GROWTH THAT MAKES SENSE

We have an excellent quality of life in Alpharetta, and any decision made by Council should be about making it even better. When we build, we need to ensure we are getting the best deal possible for our city from the property developer. Katie will ensure that new projects are first and foremost about improving the Alpharetta experience, using our leverage to secure projects that benefit the community.

LOW TAXES, SENSIBLE BUDGETS

Our City Council needs to continue to be good stewards of the money entrusted to them by Alpharetta taxpayers.
