WE NEED AN EXPERIENCED LEADER

Katie has been serving Alpharetta residents for over three decades. She was appointed to the Alpharetta Tree Commission in 1991, moved to the Alpharetta Planning Commission in 1995, and finally was elected to the Fulton County Board of Education in 1998, where she served until 2022. There is no candidate more uniquely qualified to serve us on City Council than Katie Reeves.

WHO UNDERSTANDS THAT GROWTH NEEDS TO BE BALANCED

The next decade will include the redevelopment of Northpoint Mall and an opportunity set the course for the next decade of growth in Alpharetta. Any future plans should include the wishes of Alpharetta citizens, not just the desires of developers. Katie will ensure that new projects are first and foremost about improving the Alpharetta experience, using our leverage to secure sucessful projects that benefit the community.
